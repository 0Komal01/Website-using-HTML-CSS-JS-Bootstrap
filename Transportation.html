Introduction

Wisdom Valley School is located on the Rajkot-Morbi Highway, a little outside the main city area of Rajkot. But students and parents do not need to worry as there is ample transportation facility available to and from Rajkot. Timely pick-up and drop is done by the bus drivers who drive with utmost safety. The salient features of the transportation facility at Wisdom Valley School are as follows.

Features


Reasonable Fee Structure
Buses Ensure Safety Facility
Well-Furnished Interiors and RO Filtered Drinking Water
Experienced, Uniformed, and Cultured Drivers
Provisional buses for extra classes, tours, and industrial visits
Pre-Approved Extended Bus Timing to Meet Student Needs
